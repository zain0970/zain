About Us

Coffee boutique based in Kuwait serving you the finest coffee beans to enjoy for your daily coffee cup.Our local baristas know that great espresso takes more than pushing a button. We work hard to deliver great taste while also providing knowledgeable service, perfect if you just want to try something new. We aim to provide for every taste and lifestyle with the best local ingredients through freshly baked goodies! And as a final quick intro to “us”- Our first place opened in 2010 at GUST university and provided us with real world experience to make sure B's Cafe can actually deliver what we promise- a great coffee shop experience.

Quiet Zone

this cafe offers a quite zone for students, it is a noise free space that is meant to help students focus in studying while serving them to their greatest desire .it is open from 8:00am to 10:00pm. You can place your orders in the quite zone through the ipad, and our staff with help deliver your orders quietly without disturbing you.
